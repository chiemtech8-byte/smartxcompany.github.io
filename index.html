About us

SmartX stands for Smart Everything. It’s a fusion of smart ideas, smart services, and smart living. The “X” represents endless possibilities under one platform. Founded with a vision to create opportunity and simplify life, SmartX empowers creators, business owners, learners, and everyday customers with a single trusted hub to explore, shop, learn, grow, and connect.

Our mission

To build an all-in-one digital platform that delivers quality products, essential services, and creative solutions that improve everyday life, support personal growth, and create opportunities for people across Malawi and beyond.

Our vision

To become one of Africa’s most trusted and impactful digital ecosystems — a place where ideas grow, businesses shine, artists thrive, and customers find everything they need in one smart planet.
